Personalised Guidance Plans

Basic

5$ / session

In this plan you will be alloted a personal mentor for 2 hours with whom you can discuss your doubts regarding your career. The guide will be proficient with all the fields and will try his best to understand the concerns of students and solve their doubts so that they can choose the best career for them.

Key features:
  • Personal mentor assigned
  • 2 hours mentoring session

Pro

9$ / session

In this plan you will be given a test and a mentor will be appointed who personally go through all the responses of the student and provide a personalised feedback. This will help on the spot clearing of doubts and providing a specific career for the student to choose.

Key features:
  • Personal mentor assigned
  • 2 hours mentoring session
  • On the spot career test
  • Feedback of test given by mentor

Premium

15$ / week

In this plan a personalised mentor will be assigned to the student. The mentor will conduct sessions for the student for better understanding of each profession. Then the student can approach professionals of particular profession he/she is intrested in. At the end he can give a career test for his/her suggested career.

Key features:
  • Personal mentor assigned
  • 1 weekof unlimited mentoring session

  • Feedback of test given by mentor
